# Privacy Policy for MORPHY

**Last updated: February 15, 2026**

## Introduction

MORPHY ("we", "our", or "us") is an autonomous training decision engine for serious athletes. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- Name and email address
- Age, weight, and biological sex (for training calculations)
- Training goals and fitness history

### Information From Connected Services
- Activity data from Garmin Connect and/or Strava (workouts, heart rate, pace, distance, elevation)
- Sleep and recovery data
- Historical training data

### Information Collected Automatically
- App usage data and interaction patterns
- Device type and operating system

## How We Use Your Information

We use your data exclusively to:
- Calculate your physiological state and training readiness
- Generate personalized training decisions and recommendations
- Improve our decision engine accuracy over time
- Communicate important updates about the service

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell your personal data to third parties. We only share data when:
- You explicitly authorize a third-party integration (e.g., Garmin, Strava)
- Required by law

## Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted storage and secure API communications.

## Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

## Third-Party Services

MORPHY integrates with third-party fitness platforms (Garmin Connect, Strava). Your use of these services is governed by their respective privacy policies.

## Children's Privacy

MORPHY is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes via email or in-app notification.
